Granny's Poundcake

Description

A delicious, southern staple. Sweet cake with crust that falls right off. Top it off with fresh, cold strawberries and whipped cream for a tasty dessert.

This recipe is a family favorite. Passed down from my grandmother, Gaynelle Wigley.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 3 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup oil
  • dash of salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 cups sugar

Directions

  1. Spray bundt pan with cooking spray and coat with sugar to ensure a nice crust
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  3. While oven is preheating, beat eggs, oil, and vanilla extract for several minutes
  4. After beating several minutes, add sugar
  5. Sift dry ingredients
  6. Add dry ingredients to egg mixture and stir until mixed
  7. Bake for 1 hour
