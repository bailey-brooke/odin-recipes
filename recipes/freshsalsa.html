Fresh Salsa

Description

Chips and salsa are a go-to in our house. A fresh bowl of salsa is a great snack or side dish.

A great thing about salsa is you can adjust it to make it as spicy as you would like.

Ingredients

  • 5-7 ripe Roma tomatoes
  • 1 large purple or sweet onion
  • 2-6 serrano chiles
  • fresh cilantro
  • 1 lemon
  • 1-2 limes
  • 1 tbsp. vinegar
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Mix diced tomatoes, onions, and serrano chiles in a fairly large bowl
  2. Pinch in some chopped cilantro
  3. Squeeze in lemon and lime juice
  4. Add vinegar, salt, and pepper to suit your own taste
  5. Mix well or gently stir in a blender
  6. Makes 4-6 cups of delicious, fresh salsa, and keeps well in a refrigerated closed container
