Barbecups

Description

Barbecups are a delicious dinner the whole family will enjoy. A quick choice for busy nights.

Warm, flaky biscuits with a savory filling of barbecue flavored meat which can be enjoyed with a side of salad and fries.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 to 2 tbsp packed brown sugar
  • 1 can (12 oz) biscuits
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Spray 10 regular size muffin cups with cooking spray
  3. Cook beef over medium heat until thoroughly cooked; drain
  4. Stir in barbecue sauce, onion, and brown sugar. Cook 1 minute to blend flavors.
  5. Separate dough into 10 biscuits. Place 1 biscuit in each muffin cup. Firmly press in bottom and up the sides forming a rim over the edge of the cup.
  6. Spoon about 1/4 cup beef mixture into each biscuit-lined cup.
  7. Sprinkle with cheese.
  8. Bake 10-12 minutes or until edges of biscuit are golden brown.
  9. Cool 1 minute, remove from muffin cups and enjoy!
